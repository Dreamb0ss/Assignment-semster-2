Overview

Minecraft is a sandbox game where players explore a block-based world, gather resources, craft tools, build shelters, and create anything they imagine.

Key Information

Genre: Sandbox, Survival, Adventure

Developer: Mojang Studios

Publisher: Xbox Game Studios

Release: 2011

Creative Team

Minecraft was originally created by Markus Persson and later developed by Mojang Studios.

Characters

  • Steve: Main default player character
  • Alex: Another playable character
  • Creeper: Famous enemy in the game
  • Ender Dragon: Final boss of Minecraft
