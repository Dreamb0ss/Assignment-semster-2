Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a rich and aromatic rice dish made with spices, tender chicken, and basmati rice. Perfect for special occasions and family meals.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 cup yoghurt
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Method

  1. Wash and soak rice for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat oil and fry onions until golden.
  3. Add chicken, tomatoes, yoghurt, and spices.
  4. Cook until chicken is tender.
  5. Boil rice until half cooked.
  6. Layer rice and chicken in a pot.
  7. Cook on low heat for 15 minutes.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
